Second Competition

for the best short viral video
deadline: April 6th, 2020 at 11pm



Challenges in the Coronavirus Time

Topics

  • DO RESPECT DISTANCES: SOCIAL DISTANCING
  • DON’T TOUCH YOUR FACE
  • FLATTEN THE CURVE
  • STAY AT HOME: DON’T GIVE UP
  • DISTANT, BUT CLOSE
  • THAT’S SOMETHING I WOULD NEVER HAVE EXPECTED!
  • TRUST IN THE COVID-19 ERA


Categories and prizes

category A: 14-24 years old

1st Prize 400 CHF
2nd Prize 300 CHF
3rd Prize 200 CHF
SPECIAL Prize 300 CHF for or hospital staffs' children (free topic)


category B: >24 years old

SINGLE Prize 400 CHF



Key Features

The videos must:

  • contain at least one of the proposed topics
  • be no longer than one minute
  • be in Italian or French or German or English (or with subtitles in one of these languages)
  • be in a format that can be broadcast on social media
  • start with #piuvelocidelvirus and a video title and contain the hashtag #fasterthanthevirus
  • contain references or credits and respect the copyright of any cited texts, tracks and images
  • be unpublished
  • be made whilst respecting the rules of social distancing, with people you’re at home with or in remote collaboration.


How to upload and send your video

  • Your videos must be uploaded on Instagram and sent via email / wetransfer to: piuvelocidelvirus@gmail.com by Monday, April 6th at 11.00 pm
  • In the email to piuvelocidelvirus@gmail.com, you should indicate: your age; the name under which you want it to be displayed and potentially published on the site www.piuvelocidelvirus.com or in the media. Please indicate your authorization to release the video.


It is recommended

  • to tag @piuvelocidelvirus (without accents) and @fasterthanthevirus on Instagram (from a public profile)
  • to post and share your video, as soon as it is ready, using other hashtags as well
  • to follow the Instagram account #piuvelocidelvirus


Jury

The jury will award the prizes to the videos without tolerating any form of objection. The prizes will be given to the videos it deems best, most effective and with the potential to go viral. The jury will take into account the consistency with the criteria, communicative strength, originality, quality. In addition, the jury will consider the videos’ potential to go viral and the number of likes received in the first 24 hours after the deadline of the competition.
The jury will be composed of people with different ages, geographical origins, education and jobs.



Deadline and delivery

The jury will evaluate all the videos uploaded on Instagram and sent via email / wetransfer to piuvelocidelvirus@gmail.com by Monday 6 April at 11pm.
